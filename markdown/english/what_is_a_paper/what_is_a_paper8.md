## What is a model?

<div class="ps-two-col">

  <div class="ps-tile ps-data-type">
    <h4><i class="bi bi-diagram-3-fill"></i> A Scientific Model</h4>
    <div class="ps-muted">A simplified representation of reality</div>
    <div class="ps-subcard">
      <div class="ps-subtitle">Examples</div>
      <div>Animal models, cell cultures, computational simulations, statistical models.</div>
    </div>
    <div class="ps-subcard">
      <div class="ps-subtitle">Purpose</div>
      <div>Isolate variables, test mechanisms, and make complex systems manageable.</div>
    </div>
  </div>

  <div class="ps-tile ps-data-type">
    <h4><i class="bi bi-exclamation-circle"></i> Important Limitation</h4>
    <div class="ps-muted">Models are approximations</div>
    <div class="ps-subcard">
      <div class="ps-subtitle">Example</div>
      <div>A mouse model may mimic genetic aspects of disease, but cannot replicate the full human experience.</div>
    </div>
    <div class="ps-subcard">
      <div class="ps-subtitle">Key Idea</div>
      <div>A good model captures what matters for the hypothesis, while acknowledging what it leaves out.</div>
    </div>
  </div>

</div>

## Limitations

<div class="ps-myths">

  <div class="ps-myth">
    <div class="ps-myth-icon"><i class="bi bi-check-circle-fill"></i></div>
    <div class="ps-myth-body">
      <div class="ps-myth-title">
        Every study has <span class="ps-highlight-term">constraints</span>.
      </div>
      <div class="ps-myth-text">
        Time, funding, sample access, available tools, and ethical boundaries
        all shape what is possible.
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>

  <div class="ps-myth">
    <div class="ps-myth-icon"><i class="bi bi-check-circle-fill"></i></div>
    <div class="ps-myth-body">
      <div class="ps-myth-title">
        Strong research is <span class="ps-highlight-term">transparent about limitations</span>.
      </div>
      <div class="ps-myth-text">
        Good science identifies weaknesses, minimises them where possible,
        and avoids overclaiming.
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>

</div>
