<div class="ps-block ps-callout">
  <div class="ps-callout-title">
    <i class="bi bi-lightbulb-fill"></i>
    Main Concept
  </div>
    Open Science is a movement that is challenging the <i>status quo</i>, moving away from the <span class="ps-highlight-term">publish or perish</span> pressure, encouraging <span class="ps-highlight-term">collaborative processes</span>, and <span class="ps-highlight-term">complete transparency</span>. Open Science practices are built on the idea that science is a global public good, not a competition. Of course, researchers still want to stand out in their field, but that recognition can come from rigorous methods, careful analysis, reproducibility, and meaningful contribution rather than sheer publication count.
</div>

<div class="ps-divider"></div>

## Some Open Science Practices

<div class="ps-three-col">

  <div class="ps-tile ps-data-type">
    <h4><i class="bi bi-share-fill"></i> Transparency</h4>
    <div class="ps-muted">Make your work visible</div>
    <div class="ps-subcard">
      <div class="ps-subtitle">Data & Code Sharing</div>
      <div>Allow others to inspect, verify, and reuse your work.</div>
    </div>
    <div class="ps-subcard">
      <div class="ps-subtitle">Transparent Reporting</div>
      <div>Clearly describe methods, assumptions, and limitations.</div>
    </div>
  </div>

  <div class="ps-tile ps-data-type">
    <h4><i class="bi bi-clipboard-check"></i> Pre-Registration</h4>
    <div class="ps-muted">Plan before analysing</div>
    <div class="ps-subcard">
      <div class="ps-subtitle">Study Design</div>
      <div>Document hypotheses and methods before collecting data.</div>
    </div>
    <div class="ps-subcard">
      <div class="ps-subtitle">Analysis Plans</div>
      <div>Reduce flexibility that can inflate false positives.</div>
    </div>
  </div>

  <div class="ps-tile ps-data-type">
    <h4><i class="bi bi-eye-fill"></i> Open Evaluation</h4>
    <div class="ps-muted">Increase accountability</div>
    <div class="ps-subcard">
      <div class="ps-subtitle">Pre-prints</div>
      <div>Share findings quickly before formal peer review.</div>
    </div>
    <div class="ps-subcard">
      <div class="ps-subtitle">Open Peer Review</div>
      <div>Make reviewer feedback visible and transparent.</div>
    </div>
  </div>

</div>
